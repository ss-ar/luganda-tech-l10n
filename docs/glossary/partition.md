## Meaning

A logical division of a disk used to organize storage space.

## Notes

Standard Luganda phonetic adaptation.

Use **Patisoni** consistently.
Do not use alternative spellings such as:

* Partition
* Patishoni
* Patison

Must always refer to a disk division in technical contexts.

## Examples

* English: "Create a new partition."

* Luganda: "Tonda patisoni empya."

* English: "Delete this partition."

* Luganda: "Sazaamu patisoni eno."

## Alternatives (optional)

No native Luganda equivalent approved due to length and ambiguity.
