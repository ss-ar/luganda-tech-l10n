## Meaning

A physical or virtual storage device used to store data.

## Notes

Standard Luganda phonetic adaptation.

Use **Disiki** consistently.
Do not use alternative spellings such as:

* Disk
* Diski
* Disky

Consistency across all projects is required.

## Examples

* Luganda: "Londawo disiki."
